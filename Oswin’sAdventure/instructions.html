Oswin’s Adventure

Objective: The objective of this game is to get the key and unlock the door to move to the next level.

Arrow keys: Use the arrow keys to move the character around the game board.

Key: You need the key to be able to unlock the door and jump on the ice.

Enemy: There will be at least one enemy on the board. You need to dodge the enemy. If you hit the enemy, you will lose a life.

Lives: You will get three lives. If you lose all of them, you will die and will need to restart the game. Your lives will not reset every level.

Hearts: You can collect hearts only if you don't have 3 hearts already.
